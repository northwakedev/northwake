How It Works

Three steps to sharper math.

  1. 01

    Pass the Test

    Take a 15-question test for each skill. Score 85% or above to unlock Speed Match for that topic.

  2. 02

    Drill the Gaps

    Spaced repetition surfaces your weak cards first. Study lessons, work through examples, and run timed drills until the answers come fast.

  3. 03

    Enter Speed Match

    Face off against bots in a timed battle. Win and your rating climbs. Lose and it drops. Your 1000-point rating is on the line every round.
